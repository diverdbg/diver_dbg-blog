Just cut off my social circle

2026-03-15

This is the first blog entry, and the action which made me start the blog overall.
I just cut off my social circle. Figured out during the last few months that their presence counts too much when it comes to my happiness, and that won't do.
I want to learn to be happy when alone as well. I want to enjoy things in solitude, without needing a second voice.
This is more of a test entry in any case. Thanks.
